Ethical

  • Toxicity: Product is unstable by itself. Manipulation of genes with vectors may transform the toxicity of some elements by random. No pre-market toxicity testing for product.
  • Allergic Reactions: The transfer of genes can also transfer allergens into a medium that previously had no trace of the allergens. Unknowing people could ingest these newly transferred allergens and suffer serious medical effects. Most significant population affected is children.
  • Antibiotic Resistance: Could make disease causing bacteria resistant to current antibiotics due to large scale antibiotic exposure these bacteria used as gene transfer mediums have.
